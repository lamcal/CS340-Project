Overview

The Base’ic Store is an online delivery-based grocery store that services a local area population of 100 customers. On average the store fulfills 10 to 20 orders a day with an average order cost between $200 to $500. The Base’ic Store requires customers to register a free account with provided name, email, and address before ordering any product. Payment for orders is accepted in 2 forms, either credit card or PayPal. The Base’ic Store prides itself on delivering orders to customers within 24 hours, including weekends and holidays. Customers can track their order status through the website. Order statuses will be in one of 3 stages, ‘order processing’, ‘in transit’, ‘complete’.

A database driven website will keep records of customer information and their respective orders. Customer information (name, email, and address) will be associated with a specific customer’s account. This will expedite future orders as well as maintain a record of order hister associated with said customer. Orders placed will be able to update the product stock to reflect remaining inventory of said product. Products entity will store data of a given product’s name, price, and remaining stock in store. A deliveries entity will record the order date, status, and unique id which can be used to track a customer’s current and previous orders. The suppliers entity will record the various establishments that supply the Base’ic Store with its listed product and can keep track of the store’s expenses as it relates to product acquisition.
